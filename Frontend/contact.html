What is this?

StackDepot right now is a script that is able to download all of the tasks you have completed from your RIEPS account. You provide your username, password, email, and your access code, and the script grabs each completed task from your highschool years, takes a screen shot, and downloads the files. Within 5 minutes*, you will receive an email, with a link to download the zip folder containing all tasks.


*Could take up to 15 minutes to receive the email, depending on (not limited to) the number of accounts in the queue, and/or server load.
